Based on May 2022 SNOMED CT.
Extra synonyms for 10,000 most common disorder concepts generated using synonyms of SNOMED anatomic sites and ancestors of index concept, and adding left / right concepts.

Patterns are MetaCat templated phrases using artificial random data.

Blacklist and regex updated 20 Feb 2024 after initial testing,
prior to rolling out live MiADE to all clinicians in trial. 

(Currently not extracting laterality)

cdb_problems.csv upated 11 Oct 2024 to capitalise acronyms, exclude events and non-included findings, and also require disambiguation (name_status = 'N') for two and three character concepts. Paragraph chunking regex updated on 24 Jan 2025 to include an additional version ('relevant issues') used in some ward round notes.
